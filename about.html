About Us

Who We Are
  • RougeCodes is a full-stack digital product agency founded by Bikesh Kumar and powered by a passionate team of developers, designers, and technology leaders.
  • We specialize in building scalable web applications, intuitive UI/UX systems, and advanced IoT-based prototypes for businesses, startups, and institutions.
  • We believe great products are built through clarity, creativity, and continuous innovation.
Our Story
Started as a small student-led tech initiative at IIT Patna, RougeCodes has grown into a professional product engineering agency trusted by startups and founders across India. From developing web platforms to creating ESP32-based intelligent devices, our journey is driven by passion, consistency, and a strong understanding of real-world problems.
Our Vision
To become India's most trusted and innovative technology agency — delivering solutions that empower businesses, enhance lives, and shape the future.
