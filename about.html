The Vision.

Founded by Bikesh Kumar, RougeCodes is a full-stack digital collective powered by passion and engineering excellence.

Started at IIT Patna, we grew from a student initiative into a professional agency. We specialize in building scalable web applications and advanced IoT systems for institutions and startups.

Our goal is simple: To become India's most trusted technology partner by delivering products that actually work under pressure.
